The first live role playing game
in the Philippines

Mystery Manila is an activity that allows people to try something new and challenging. Choose between several mysteries that will transport you to various themed locations and scenarios. You will discover different clues and objects that will help you solve the mystery. It's up to you to use your wits to figure out how to escape in less than 60 minutes.

One room.
One mystery.
One hour.
One way out.

Can you solve it?
