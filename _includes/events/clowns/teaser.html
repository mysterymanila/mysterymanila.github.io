ARE YOU UP FOR THE CHALLENGE?

The carnival was abandoned, left to rot but the clowns never left. Cursed by the Tragedy, their spirits lingered, their once joyful laughter now a sinister echo in the Dark. The carnival reappears once every halloween, drawing unsuspecting victims Into its haunted grounds.

Those who enter are greeted by the clowns and tasked to complete all the Challenges along the way to escape and defeat the clown. So, are you up for the Challenge?
