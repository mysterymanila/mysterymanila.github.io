FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

WHEN IS THE EVENT?

The event is from September 7 - September 15. Book a slot anytime between 2pm - 9pm during weekdays or 10am - 10pm on Weekends and Holidays

WHERE IS THE EVENT?

The Tent at Solaire. 1 Asean Avenue Tambo Entertainment City, Parañaque, 1701 Metro Manila.

HOW MUCH PER HEAD?

It’s 500 per head.

HOW DO I BOOK?:

HOW MANY PLAYERS IN A GROUP?

The game is designed for a minimum of 2 up to a maximum of 5 players.

REFUND POLICY

Paid bookings cannot be refunded but may be transferred to a different date/time slot subject to our rebooking fees and policies.

HOW LONG IS THE GAME:

Mad King's Murder - 20 mins
Deadly Dungeion - 30 mins

What happens if I don't get to solve it?

If your time is up and you haven’t solved the mystery, you have the option of trying it again at a discounted rate. This will be subject to booking availability.

Can I get a clue or seek help during the game?

Yes but every clue requested will equate to a penalty of 5 minute addition to your total solving time.

Age limit:

Groups with minors below 18 years old should be accompanied by at least 1 adult.

What if I really want to play alone?

Depending on the mystery, you can play alone if you really want to but you have to pay for the price of two players. The mysteries are designed to be solved by at least two players and a maximum of 5. We believe that it is better and much more fun to solve mysteries together.
